Adopted by GOA in the late 90’s, pantiestheclown has been antagonizing trance floors for over 20 years. Using unsound & unorthodox methods, he crafts a dance floor strictly meant for working shit out. Pushing boundaries, testing limits and operating without rules are the core foundation of his madness. When the chaos engine ignites, the portal opens and you find yourself wondering “should I dance or throw up?”, just remember……….. He’s just a fucking clown.
